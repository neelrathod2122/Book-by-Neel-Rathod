She Left, I Wrote

by Neel Rathod

About the Book

This book is a heartfelt reflection on love, loss, and the unspoken emotions that linger when a story remains unfinished. Through deeply personal Hindi shayaris, each moment is captured -- from the innocence of first love to the ache of separation. Each verse is paired with its English explanation, bridging languages but never dulling the pain or beauty.

"If I had found you the story would have ended, now that I have lost you the story will definitely be long..."

About the Author

Neel Rathod is a passionate writer who finds meaning in emotions often left unsaid. With a heart full of poetry and a mind shaped by real experiences, he brings raw stories to life -- not through fantasy, but truth. This book is a piece of his soul, offered to every reader who has ever loved, lost, and still found the strength to write.
